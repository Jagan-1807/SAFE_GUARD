# SafeGuard AI - Support Documents

## Company Refund Policy (ID: REF-001)
*   Customers can request a full refund within **30 days** of purchase.
*   The product must be in its original packaging.
*   Refunds take **5-7 business days** to process.
*   Software licenses are **not refundable** once the key has been activated.

## Shipping & Delivery (ID: SHP-002)
*   Standard shipping takes **3-5 business days**.
*   Express shipping is available for **$15.00** and takes **1-2 business days**.
*   We ship internationally to over **50 countries**.
*   Tracking numbers are provided via email within **24 hours** of dispatch.

## Technical Support (ID: TECH-003)
*   Our support team is available **Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST**.
*   For urgent issues, use the **Live Chat** on our website.
*   Email support response time is typically within **12 hours**.

## Account Security (ID: SEC-004)
*   Users must enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** for all accounts containing sensitive data.
*   Passwords must be at least **12 characters** long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
*   Account recovery requires valid ID verification.
